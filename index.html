How to Play

Objective

Match all 8 pairs of programming language cards to win!

Rules

  1. Click any card to flip and reveal the language
  2. Click a second card to find its pair
  3. Match = Points earned! Mismatch = Cards flip back
  4. Match all 8 pairs to win

Combo System

Chain matches for bonus points:

  • 1st match: 100 pts
  • 2x combo: 150 pts (+50)
  • 3x combo: 200 pts (+100)
  • 4x+ combo: +50 pts per level

Warning: Missing resets combo to 0!

Pro Tips

  • Remember card positions for longer combos
  • Accuracy matters more than speed
  • Beat your high score!
